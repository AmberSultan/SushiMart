Spider Roll

Salmon Avocado Roll

The Salmon Avocado Roll typically contains nori (seaweed sheet), sushi rice, fresh salmon (raw or cooked), and avocado.

Dragonfly Roll

The Dragonfly Roll typically includes shrimp tempura, avocado, cucumber, crab stick (or imitation crab meat), tobiko (flying fish roe), nori (seaweed), and sushi rice.
